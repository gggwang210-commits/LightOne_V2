# LIGHT ONE Safety and Privacy Policy

LIGHT ONE is a non-medical wellness and exercise counseling support service. It helps trainers organize exercise session records, RPE, pain response records, and trainer notes for counseling workflows. It does not provide medical diagnosis, disease prediction, treatment, rehabilitation treatment, or prescription.

## Safety boundaries

- Use “pain response record” language instead of pain-cause analysis.
- Keep AI/system outputs as trainer review aids, not final decisions.
- Treat AUTO / REVIEW / BLOCK routing as workflow triage, not clinical judgment.
- Stop-session or BLOCK wording must avoid diagnosis and may recommend professional consultation when appropriate.

## Privacy principles

- Collect only the minimum data needed for customer validation and pilot operation.
- Do not commit real member personal information, photos, videos, or sensitive health information to the public repository.
- Store validation evidence as de-identified summaries when shared in GitHub, Slack, or submission materials.
- Use synthetic sample data for demos, screenshots, and CI tests.
